Important Disclaimers

This calculator provides an *estimate* of severe weather risk based on mathematical equations. It is not a substitute for official weather forecasts and warnings.

Data used in this calculator may contain inaccuracies. Always consult multiple sources for weather information.

Use this tool at your own risk. The creators are not responsible for any decisions made based on its output.

Tornado formation is a complex process, and this calculator cannot predict tornadoes with certainty.

Please exercise caution and prioritize your safety during severe weather events.

It is *recommended* that you use HRRR or other short-range models for data input, as long range models (such as GFS) can have increased inaccuracies.

Important Data Input Process **READ**

For CAPE: Use Surface CAPE

For Wind Shear: Use Bulk Shear: 0-6 km AGL

For Helicity: Use SRH: Use 0-3 km AGL

For Relative Humidity: Use 2m AGL RH

For Dew Point: Use 2m AGL Dew Point

For DCAPE: Use DCAPE
